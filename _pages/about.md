My name is Leanna (she/her) and I am a third-year PhD candidate in psychology and the collaborative specialization in neuroscience program at the University of Toronto. My dissertation research is focused on how social neuropeptides (i.e., oxytocin and vasopressin) regulate the response to social stress in eusocial naked mole-rats. I am incredibly interested in how fundamental science can be better leveraged to inform public policy, and am currently heavily involved with the American Psychological Association, Toronto Science Policy Network, and National Science Policy Network. I am also currently a policy intern with the Research-to-Policy Collaboration at Penn State. 

Feel free to connect if you'd like to chat about science policy and/or collaborate on something!
